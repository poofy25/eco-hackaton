# ReMatero — User Personas

## Seller Personas

---

### S1: Andrei — Construction Company Manager

**Profile**
- **Age:** 42 | **Location:** București
- **Role:** Operations Manager at a mid-size construction firm (50+ employees)
- **Tech comfort:** Medium — uses ERP, email, WhatsApp daily; comfortable with web apps

**Goals**
- Recover costs from surplus materials instead of paying disposal fees
- Maintain a clean, organized construction site
- Build company reputation as environmentally responsible

**Pain Points**
- Currently pays 2,000-5,000 RON/month for waste disposal of usable materials
- Posting on Facebook groups is time-consuming and unprofessional
- No way to bulk-list materials or manage multiple listings efficiently
- Worried about liability — what if sold materials cause problems?

**Typical Scenario**
> *"Am terminat proiectul de pe Calea Victoriei și mi-au rămas 3 paleți de cărămidă, 200m² de gresie și 50 de saci de ciment. Pe Facebook am pus anunț și am primit 40 de mesaje, jumătate întrebau 'mai e valabil?' și nimeni nu a venit. Am ajuns să arunc totul."*
>
> *(We finished the project on Calea Victoriei and had 3 pallets of bricks, 200m² of tiles, and 50 bags of cement left. I posted on Facebook and got 40 messages — half asking 'is it still available?' and nobody showed up. I ended up throwing everything away.)*

**What ReMatero offers Andrei**
- Bulk listing tool — list multiple materials from one project quickly
- Professional company profile with verification badge
- Reservation system — only serious buyers who commit
- Transaction history for accounting/compliance

### S2: Elena — Renovation Contractor

**Profile**
- **Age:** 35 | **Location:** Cluj-Napoca
- **Role:** Independent renovation contractor, 3-person team
- **Tech comfort:** High — manages business via phone, uses apps for everything

**Goals**
- Minimize waste from each project to improve margins
- Find buyers quickly — materials clutter her limited storage
- Build a reputation and get referrals

**Pain Points**
- Small quantities that don't justify posting on marketplaces
- Materials vary project to project — no consistent inventory
- Time is her scarcest resource; can't spend hours negotiating
- Clients sometimes want her to "get rid of" leftover materials

**Typical Scenario**
> *"După fiecare renovare am câte puțin din toate — 2m² de parchet, resturi de vopsea, niște prize și întrerupătoare noi. Sunt bune, dar cui le vând? Nu merită să pierd o zi pentru 200 de lei."*
>
> *(After every renovation I have a bit of everything — 2m² of parquet, leftover paint, some new sockets and switches. They're good, but who do I sell them to? It's not worth losing a day for 200 lei.)*

**What ReMatero offers Elena**
- Quick listing (< 2 minutes per item with photo + category selection)
- Location-based matching — nearby buyers come to her
- "Lot mixt" (mixed lot) listing option — bundle leftovers from one project
- Rating system that builds her professional reputation

### S3: Marius — Demolition Firm Owner

**Profile**
- **Age:** 50 | **Location:** Timișoara
- **Role:** Owner of a demolition and salvage company (15 employees)
- **Tech comfort:** Low-Medium — relies on phone calls, basic smartphone use

**Goals**
- Maximize revenue from salvaged materials before disposal
- Find buyers for large quantities quickly (time-sensitive, site deadlines)
- Document material provenance for regulatory compliance

**Pain Points**
- Large volumes that need to move fast — a demolition site can't wait
- Quality assessment is subjective — buyers don't trust descriptions
- Transport logistics are a major barrier for heavy materials
- No standardized way to describe salvaged material condition

**Typical Scenario**
> *"Demolăm o fabrică veche și avem tone de oțel, cărămidă, lemn de stejar din grinzi. Am sunat 20 de oameni, 3 au venit să vadă, unul a luat oțelul la preț de nimic. Restul a ajuns la groapă."*
>
> *(We're demolishing an old factory and have tons of steel, bricks, oak beams. I called 20 people, 3 came to look, one took the steel for almost nothing. The rest went to the dump.)*

**What ReMatero offers Marius**
- "Listing urgent" feature — time-sensitive listings get priority visibility
- Verified certification — professional inspection validates material quality
- Bulk listing with tonnage estimates
- Future: logistics partner integration for heavy transport

## Buyer Personas

---

### B1: Cristina — Individual Renovator

**Profile**
- **Age:** 32 | **Location:** Brașov
- **Role:** Young professional renovating her first apartment
- **Tech comfort:** High — shops online regularly, uses comparison apps

**Goals**
- Save 30-50% on renovation materials
- Find specific items that match her design vision (reclaimed wood, vintage tiles)
- Reduce environmental footprint of her renovation

**Pain Points**
- New materials are expensive; renovation budget is tight
- Finding second-hand construction materials requires knowing "the right people"
- Afraid of buying poor-quality materials that look good in photos
- Doesn't own a truck — transport is a challenge

**Typical Scenario**
> *"Vreau parchet din lemn masiv pentru living, dar costă 200 lei/m² nou. Am văzut pe un grup de Facebook că cineva vindea parchet recuperat, dar când am ajuns era complet zgâriat și diferit de poze."*
>
> *(I want solid wood flooring for my living room, but it costs 200 lei/m² new. I saw on a Facebook group that someone was selling recovered flooring, but when I got there it was completely scratched and different from the photos.)*

**What ReMatero offers Cristina**
- Condition ratings with standardized photo requirements
- Price comparison context (% savings vs. new)
- Seller ratings and reviews from previous buyers
- Location filter — find materials close to her
- Future: transport partner booking

### B2: Dan — NGO Coordinator

**Profile**
- **Age:** 45 | **Location:** Iași
- **Role:** Program coordinator at a housing NGO (builds/repairs homes for vulnerable families)
- **Tech comfort:** Medium — uses office software, email, basic web

**Goals**
- Source materials at minimal cost (donated or heavily discounted)
- Get consistent supply for ongoing construction projects
- Document material sourcing for grant reporting

**Pain Points**
- Extremely limited budget — every RON counts
- Needs to prove material quality and safety for beneficiary homes
- Spends weeks calling around to find donated materials
- No leverage for bulk pricing as a small NGO

**Typical Scenario**
> *"Construim 5 case pentru familii defavorizate anul acesta. Bugetul pentru materiale e de 15.000 lei per casă. Dacă am găsi cărămidă și cherestea la jumătate de preț, am putea construi o casă în plus."*
>
> *(We're building 5 houses for disadvantaged families this year. The materials budget is 15,000 lei per house. If we could find bricks and lumber at half price, we could build one more house.)*

**What ReMatero offers Dan**
- "Donație" (donation) filter — find free materials
- NGO badge on profile — sellers may offer better prices
- Saved searches with notifications — get alerted when needed materials appear
- Transaction history for grant reporting and audits

---

### B3: Radu — Small Business Owner (Construction)

**Profile**
- **Age:** 38 | **Location:** Constanța
- **Role:** Owner of a small construction firm (8 employees), residential focus
- **Tech comfort:** Medium-High — uses smartphone for everything, basic accounting software

**Goals**
- Reduce material costs to win more competitive bids
- Source materials quickly — project timelines are tight
- Find reliable, recurring sources of quality surplus materials

**Pain Points**
- Competing with larger firms that get volume discounts from suppliers
- Quality uncertainty when buying second-hand
- No time to drive across the city to inspect materials that might not be what was described
- Needs invoices/receipts for accounting

**Typical Scenario**
> *"Am o lucrare de terasament și am nevoie de 500 buc BCA. Noi costă 8 lei bucata, dar am auzit că cineva pe lângă Mamaia a demolat și are BCA bun. Am trimis un om să verifice — erau sparte pe jumătate. Am pierdut o zi degeaba."*
>
> *(I have a foundation job and need 500 aerated concrete blocks. New they cost 8 lei each, but I heard someone near Mamaia demolished and has good ones. I sent a guy to check — half were broken. Lost a day for nothing.)*

**What ReMatero offers Radu**
- Verified listings with condition certification
- Quantity accuracy guarantees (verified seller badge)
- Proximity search with distance-based sorting
- Seller history and ratings — identify reliable sources
- Reservation system — lock in materials before site visit
